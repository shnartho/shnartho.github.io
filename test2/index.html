19/11/14: Mobility Program in Portugal

The ETSIT-UPM is committed to the international mobility of students. Bilateral agreements between ETSIT and the most prestigious centers on the five continents. Dual degree programs and others such as Socrates / LLP (Erasmus), Magalhaes / Smile or Athens are the options that final year students choose to complete their training abroad. The mobility offer for the 2010/2011 academic year will include the possibility that stays at the destination universities be carried out not only for one year, as up to now, but also for semesters, with the aim of being able to recognize optional credits of the degree .
